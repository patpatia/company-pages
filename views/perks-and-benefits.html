Perks and Benefits

We recognize the unique needs of professionals in the workplace and provide them with various perks and benefits

Maternity Policy
Benefits such as Paid Maternity Leave (Twenty-Six Weeks), Work from Home (WFH), along with separate Leaves for Miscarriage and Adoption
Maternity Benefit in Mediclaim Policy
Post-delivery medical benefits to all WNS Employees
Pregnancy Care Program
Social health & wellness platform that offers help to employees of WNS during pregnancy and period thereafter, to ensure a healthy baby and a healthy mother
Prevention of Sexual Harassment at Workplace
A safe and secure environment with strict anti – harassment policy
WNS Sports for Women
We encourage our women athletes to outperform through sponsorships across various sports.
