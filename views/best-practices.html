Inclusion & Diversity Week
Inclusion & Diversity week is organized every year, to promote a culture of I&D and strengthen our commitment.This includes a holistic set of events conducted across locations under different campaigns curated for the year, with focus the years I&D agenda.

New Parent Policy
GSK continues relentlessly to create an inclusive and diverse environment, empowering our employees to contribute to their full potential.In 2018, we introduced our Child Care Support Policy to our women employees and the organization tied up with Klay for crèche benefits, across locations in India.The Child Care Support policy extends to all permanent women employees in GSK. The benefit can be availed by eligible employees for a maximum of 2 children in the age bracket of 6 months to 4 years. The policy supports, eligible employees to avail crèche facility for their children.
